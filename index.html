ABOUT ME

Hey, I’m Miguel! I’m a web designer and frontend developer based in Muskegon, MI.

I have always had a love for computers, technology and video games. Originally a CAD Drafter, I found a strong interest in the world of web design. After picking up courses on freeCodeCamp and enrolling in my local community college, my interest only evolved.

I create clean and professional websites, iconic logos, and quality videos for small and medium size businesses. I will utilize WordPress or static HTML to help make your business grow. I love helping those that are starting out or are in need of a redesign. If you are in need of a website, logo or video, then let’s get in touch and make it happen.
